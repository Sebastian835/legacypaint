Who We Are

Professional Excellence Since Day One

Legacy Paint Services is a professional company dedicated to enhancing properties through expert painting and comprehensive yard care. We specialize in transforming homes and commercial spaces, providing vibrant, well-maintained, and attractive environments.

Our team combines experience, attention to detail, and reliable service to deliver results that property owners and managers can trust. Every property is treated with care and professionalism, as if it were our own.

Our Commitment to Safety

Safety is a top priority at Legacy Paint Services. We ensure the protection of our team and every property through responsible procedures and safe practices. Owner Yunexy Rodríguez is always available by phone for any urgent situation or incident that requires immediate attention.

What We Do

Comprehensive Solutions for Your Property

Painting Services

  • Interior painting (walls, ceilings, trim, baseboards)
  • Exterior painting (siding, fascia, shutters, doors)
  • Fence and deck painting or staining
  • Cabinets refinishing and repainting
  • Surface preparation (sanding, priming, caulking)
  • Drywall repair and texture matching
  • Color consultation and finish selection
  • Small carpentry touch-ups prior to painting

Yard & Lawn Care

  • Lawn mowing, edging, and blowing
  • Hedge trimming, shrub shaping & bush removal
  • Tree trimming and small tree removal
  • Mulch installation, pine straw, rock bedding
  • Flower bed cleaning, weeding & replanting
  • Seasonal cleanups (spring / fall)
  • Leaf removal and yard debris hauling
  • Pressure washing (driveways, patios, decks, fences)
  • Sod installation and lawn restoration

Remodeling & Maintenance

  • Kitchen & bathroom cabinet installation
  • Flooring installation
  • Door installation and replacement
  • Bathtub/shower installation
  • Toilet installation
  • Appliance installation
  • Basic plumbing & electrical work
  • Light fixtures & ceiling fans
  • Emergency response services
